Black Sheep RPG

[Java, JGame]

Background

This game I made back in college as part of a class where we each created our own games in java. This game is pretty simple and mimicks gameplay from games like Pokemon, the crux of which is two main layers of gameplay, which in this case, includes a main navigation layer and a battle layer.

Intro

On launch, Black Sheep RPG presents an instructions page. The goal of the game is to gain stats in the three colors (RGB) by defeating enemies.

Start Game: [Space]

Stats

By defeating enemies of a specific color, the player gains stats matching the color. As stats change, the color of the player icon changes with it -- RGB(red, green, blue).

Return to Map: [Esc]

Movement

In the game, the player (circle) moves around the map fighting enemies (squares). Each move has a chance of generating a new enemy (random colored square) in a random open square.

Movement: [Arrow Keys]
See Stats: [Esc]
Add free stats: [r/g/b]

Battle

Upon encountering an enemy, battle begins. The strength of the enemy is directly proportional to the number of the enemies on the map. The more the player walks, the stronger the enemies get!

Each attack scales with a corresponding stat or in the case of the prismatic attack, a sum of all stats.

Select Attack: [Space] Move Cursor: [Arrow Keys]
