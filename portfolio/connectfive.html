Background

Since early childhood, I have been playing a game akin to the Tic Tac Toe. It's called 五子棋 (Wuziqi) in Chinese, 五目並べ (Gomoku) in Japanese and Connect Five in English. It's a simple game that can be played on a go board or with a pencil and a piece of paper. Alternatively, players can play only verbally for a challenge in mental finesse.

Rules

The goal of the game is to connect five of your own pieces in a row horizontally, vertically or diagnonally.

Start Game: [Space]

Gameplay

The gameplay is cursor-based. Use arrow keys to move the cursor and the "z" key to place down a piece at the cursor location. Pieces must be placed in empty spaces and turns change between the two players after each piece is placed.
